## 📌 Project Overview

This project analyzes housing market trends across major European countries to identify the most attractive real estate investment opportunities using a data-driven approach.

The dashboard combines:

- Housing affordability metrics
- Market growth indicators
- Forecast modeling
- Investment scoring
- Geographic market analysis

The objective was to simulate how a Business Intelligence Analyst or Data Analyst would support investment decision-making using Python, SQL, and Tableau.

---

# 🎯 Business Problem

Real estate investors often struggle to evaluate:

- Which European housing markets offer the best investment potential
- Which markets are becoming overvalued
- Where affordability pressure is increasing
- How future market growth may affect investment attractiveness

This project transforms raw housing and economic datasets into actionable investment intelligence.

# 📈 Key Insights

## 🥇 Portugal emerged as the strongest investment market

**Investment Score:** `79.31`

Driven by:
- Strong projected growth
- High investment attractiveness
- Positive positioning relative to other European markets

---

## 🟢 Austria showed balanced market conditions

Austria demonstrated:
- Stable market performance
- Moderate affordability pressure
- Balanced long-term investment potential

---

## ⚠️ Affordability pressure is rising sharply

Countries such as:
- Portugal
- Netherlands
- Spain

showed affordability index values significantly above the OECD baseline.

This suggests increasing housing pressure relative to income levels.

---

## 📉 European market momentum is slowing

**Average Growth Momentum:** `-17.77`

This indicates:
- Market cooling across Europe
- Slowing post-boom housing growth
- Increased investment caution
